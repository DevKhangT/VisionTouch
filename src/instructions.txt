Steps to Use VisionTouch Linear Regression
------------------------------------------
1. Utilize the collect_calibration.py file to collect the complete dataset yourself.
2. There are two choices after doing the collection:
    Choice 1: 
    Try the stage 1 tests that test the differences when the dataset is exposed to IRIS_ONLY, FACE_CENTER, and HEAD_POSE;
    IRIS_ONLY is the x and y of both eyes only.
    FACE_CENTER is IRIS_ONLY with an addition of the face center x and ys 
    HEAD_POSE is FACE_CENTER with an addition of head yaw, pitch, and roll plus face width and height
    Choice 2:
    Test your dataset on the ablation tests where the different results are compared with one another.
